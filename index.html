Babar Azam

Mr Consistent

Greatest of All Time

Babar Azam, born on October 15, 1994, in Lahore, Pakistan, is a renowned cricketer known for his elegant batting style and remarkable consistency. Babar made his international debut for Pakistan in 2015 and quickly rose through the ranks to become one of the leading batsmen in world cricket. With his impeccable technique and ability to play all around the ground, Babar has established himself as the backbone of Pakistan's batting lineup across all formats of the game. He has broken numerous records and earned widespread acclaim for his batting prowess, making him one of the most exciting talents in modern cricket.

Achivements

  • Fastest to reach 1,000 T20I runs.
  • Ranked No. 1 in ICC T20I Rankings.
  • Fastest Pakistani to reach 1,000 ODI runs.
  • Fastest Pakistani to reach 1,000 T20 runs.
  • Successful captaincy, leading Pakistan to series wins in ODIs and T20Is.
